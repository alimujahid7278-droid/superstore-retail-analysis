# superstore-retail-analysis

# Project Overview 
This project analyzes the Superstore retail dataset to understand business performance, profitability trends, 
customer behavior, product performance, and the impact of discounts on profit. 
The goal of this analysis is to identify key business insights and provide recommendations that can help improve 
profitability and operational performance.

# Key Insights 
Technology category generates the highest profit. 
Furniture category shows lower profit margins compared to other categories. 
Approximately 26% of orders are loss-making. 
Higher discount levels are strongly associated with negative profit. 
Some sub-categories such as Tables and Bookcases consistently generate losses. 

# Delivery Insights 
Average delivery time is approximately 4 days. 
Late deliveries represent about 18% of total orders. 
Standard shipping is the most frequently used delivery method. 

# Business Recommendations 
Reduce excessive discount levels on low-margin products. 
Review pricing strategy for loss-making product categories. 
Focus sales and marketing efforts on high-profit categories such as Technology. 
Monitor discount policies to maintain sustainable profit margins. 
Identify and remove consistently loss-making products from the catalog.
